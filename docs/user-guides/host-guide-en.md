## Become a Host

### Why Become a Host?

Becoming a host on Campbnb Québec allows you to:
- **Generate income** by renting your camping space
- **Meet travelers** passionate about nature
- **Share your love** of camping and Québec
- **Value your land** or property

### Requirements to Become a Host

- Have an available camping space (land, private campsite, etc.)
- Comply with local regulations
- Be available to welcome and communicate with guests
- Have a verified Campbnb account

### Verification Process

1. **Create your account** or sign in
2. **Activate host mode** in settings
3. **Complete your host profile**:
- Contact information
- Identity verification
- Bank information (for payments)
4. **Create your first listing**

## Create a Listing

### Basic Information

1. **Catchy title**: Describe your campsite in a few words
- Example: "Lakeside campsite with stunning view"

2. **Detailed description**:
- Present your campsite
- Mention highlights
- Describe the environment
- Use AI to generate an optimized description

3. **Campsite type**:
- Tent
- RV (Recreational Vehicle)
- Ready-to-Camp
- Wild

### Location

1. **Full address**: Enter your address
2. **Map verification**: Verify the location is correct
3. **Nearby points of interest**: Mention local attractions

### Capacity and Amenities

- **Maximum number of guests**
- **Number of bedrooms** (if applicable)
- **Number of beds**
- **Number of bathrooms**
- **Available amenities**:
- WiFi
- Shower
- Toilets
- Fire Pit
- Potable water
- Electricity
- Parking
- And more...

### Rules and Policies

1. **House rules**:
- Quiet hours
- Pets allowed or not
- Smoking allowed or not
- Other specific rules

2. **Cancellation policy**:
- Flexible
- Moderate
- Strict

3. **Check-in/check-out times**:
- Define arrival and departure times

### Pricing

1. **Base price per night**: Set your rate
2. **Cleaning fee** (optional)
3. **Service fee**: Automatically calculated (10%)
4. **Discounts**:
- Weekly discount
- Monthly discount

### Photos

**Tips for great photos**:
- Take photos in natural light
- Show different angles of the campsite
- Include photos of amenities
- Eye-catching cover photo
- Minimum 5 photos recommended

### Publication

1. **Check all information**
2. **Preview your listing**
3. **Publish** your listing

## Manage Listings

### Edit a Listing

1. **Go to your dashboard**
2. **Select the listing** to edit
3. **Modify desired information**
4. **Save changes**

### Manage Availability

1. **Calendar**: Mark available/unavailable dates
2. **Blockings**: Block dates for maintenance or personal use
3. **Synchronization**: Sync with other calendars if needed

### Dynamic Pricing

- **Adjust prices** according to season
- **Increase prices** for popular dates
- **Offer discounts** for less requested dates

### Listing Status

- **Active**: Visible and bookable
- **Inactive**: Temporarily disabled
- **Pending**: Under moderation
- **Rejected**: Requires modifications

## Manage Reservations

### Reservation Requests

You receive notifications for each new request:

1. **Review the request**:
- Requested dates
- Number of guests
- Guest message
- Guest profile

2. **Make a decision**:
- **Accept**: Reservation is confirmed
- **Decline**: Explain the reason (optional)
- **Suggest alternative dates**: If dates don't work

3. **Response time**: You have 24 hours to respond

### Confirmed Reservations

For each confirmed reservation:
- **Complete details**: Dates, guests, price
- **Contact information**: Communicate with guest
- **Directions**: Send access instructions
- **Management**: Modify or cancel if necessary

### Check-in and Check-out

1. **Before arrival**:
- Confirm arrival time
- Send access instructions
- Verify everything is ready

2. **During stay**:
- Stay available to answer questions
- Ensure everything goes well

3. **After departure**:
- Check campsite condition
- Leave a review for the guest

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### I'm not receiving requests

- Check that your listing is active
- Check your prices (maybe too high)
- Improve your photos and description
- Check your availability

#### A guest cancelled

- Check cancellation policy
- Calendar updates automatically
- You can offer dates to other guests

#### Payment issue

- Check your bank information
- Contact support for delays
- Check your payment history
