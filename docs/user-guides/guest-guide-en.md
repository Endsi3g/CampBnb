## Getting Started

### What is Campbnb Québec?

Campbnb Québec is a campsite booking platform that allows you to:
- Discover unique campsites in Québec
- Easily book your stay
- Communicate directly with hosts
- Share your experiences

### Navigating the Application

The application is organized into main sections:
- **Home**: Discover recommended campsites
- **Search**: Find your ideal campsite
- **Map**: Explore campsites on an interactive map
- **Messages**: Communicate with hosts
- **Profile**: Manage your account and reservations

---

## Create an Account

### Sign Up

1. **Open** the Campbnb Québec app
2. **Tap "Sign Up"** on the welcome screen
3. **Choose your sign-up method**:
- **Email**: Enter your email and a secure password
- **Google**: Sign in with your Google account
- **Apple**: Sign in with your Apple account (iOS)

### Complete Your Profile

After signing up, complete your profile:
- **Profile Photo**: Add a photo so hosts can get to know you
- **Full Name**: Your first and last name
- **Phone**: To facilitate communication
- **Bio**: Briefly introduce yourself (optional)

### Verification

- Verify your email if you signed up with email
- Complete the onboarding to discover features

## Search for a Campsite

### Simple Search

1. **Go to the search screen**
2. **Type your destination** or use voice search
3. **Select your stay dates**
4. **Indicate the number of guests**
5. **Tap "Search"**

### Intelligent AI Search

Use intelligent search for personalized suggestions:
- **"Campsite near a lake for fishing"**
- **"Campsite with shower for family"**
- **"Isolated campsite for meditation"**

The Gemini AI analyzes your request and suggests the best matching campsites.

### Advanced Filters

Refine your search with filters:
- **Campsite Type**: Tent, RV, Ready-to-Camp, Wild
- **Price**: Price range per night
- **Amenities**: WiFi, Shower, Toilets, Fire Pit, etc.
- **Minimum Rating**: Campsites with a minimum rating
- **Distance**: Proximity to a city or point of interest

### Map Search

1. **Go to the Map screen**
2. **Visually explore** available campsites
3. **Tap a marker** to see details
4. **Use filters** directly on the map

## Make a Reservation

### View Details

1. **Select a campsite** from the results
2. **Review the information**:
- Photos and description
- Available amenities
- House rules
- Reviews from other guests
- Location on the map

### Check Availability

- The calendar shows available dates (in green) and unavailable dates (grayed out)
- Already booked dates are marked

### Book

1. **Select your check-in and check-out dates**
2. **Indicate the number of guests** (adults and children)
3. **Review the total price**:
- Price per night
- Cleaning fee
- Service fee
- Taxes
4. **Add a message** for the host (optional)
5. **Tap "Book"**

### Confirmation

- Your reservation request is sent to the host
- You receive a confirmation notification
- The host has 24 hours to confirm or decline

### Payment

- Payment is secured via Stripe
- You will only be charged after host confirmation
- If declined, no charges will be made

## Manage Reservations

### My Reservations

Access your reservations from your profile:
- **Upcoming**: Confirmed upcoming reservations
- **Pending**: Requests awaiting confirmation
- **Past**: Your stay history
- **Cancelled**: Cancelled reservations

### Available Actions

**For a pending reservation:**
- Cancel the request
- Modify dates (if host allows)
- Contact the host

**For a confirmed reservation:**
- View complete details
- Get directions
- Contact the host
- Cancel (according to cancellation policy)

### Cancellation Policy

Each campsite has its own cancellation policy:
- **Flexible**: Full refund up to 24 hours before
- **Moderate**: Full refund up to 5 days before
- **Strict**: 50% refund up to 7 days before

Check details when booking.

## Messaging

### Contact a Host

1. **From a campsite page**: Tap "Contact host"
2. **From your reservations**: Access the conversation
3. **From the Messages tab**: Create a new conversation

### Conversation

- **Real-time messages**: Receive responses instantly
- **Notifications**: Be alerted of new messages
- **History**: Keep all your conversations

### Best Practices

- Ask questions before booking
- Respond quickly to host messages
- Be respectful and courteous

## ⭐ Leave a Review

### After Your Stay

1. **Go to your past reservations**
2. **Select the completed stay**
3. **Tap "Leave a review"**
4. **Rate the campsite** on several criteria:
- Overall rating
- Cleanliness
- Communication
- Check-in
- Accuracy of listing
- Location
- Value for money
5. **Write a detailed comment**
6. **Publish your review**

### Tips for a Good Review

- Be honest and constructive
- Mention positive points and possible improvements
- Respect host privacy
- Avoid personal information

## Tips and Tricks

### To Find the Ideal Campsite

1. **Use intelligent search**: AI helps you find exactly what you're looking for
2. **Read reviews**: Other guests' reviews are very helpful
3. **Check the map**: Visualize location and surroundings
4. **Contact the host**: Ask questions before booking

### For a Better Experience

1. **Book in advance**: Best campsites go fast
2. **Read the rules**: Respect house rules
3. **Communicate**: Stay in touch with the host
4. **Be punctual**: Respect check-in/check-out times

### Security

- **Never share** your payment information outside the platform
- **Communicate only** via Campbnb messaging
- **Report any suspicious behavior** to support

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### I'm not receiving notifications

1. Check notification settings in the app
2. Check your phone's notification settings
3. Make sure you're connected to the internet

#### I can't make a reservation

- Check that dates are available
- Check that you've filled all required fields
- Check your payment method

#### Host is not responding

- Wait 24 hours for a response
- Send a reminder message
- Contact support if necessary

#### Payment issue

- Check that your card is valid
- Contact your bank
- Contact Campbnb support

### Contact Support

- **Email**: support@campbnb.quebec
- **Chat**: Available in the app
- **FAQ**: Check the [Guest FAQ](../faq/faq-guest-en.md)
