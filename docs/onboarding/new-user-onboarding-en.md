# New User Onboarding - Campbnb Québec

Welcome to Campbnb Québec! This guide accompanies you during your first use of the platform.

## Objective

By the end of this onboarding, you will be ready to:
- Navigate the application
- Search for campsites
- Make your first reservation
- Use all main features

---

## Step 1: Download and Install

### 1.1 Download

**iOS:**
1. Open the App Store
2. Search for "Campbnb Québec"
3. Tap "Get"

**Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "Campbnb Québec"
3. Tap "Install"

### 1.2 First Open

1. Tap the Campbnb Québec icon
2. Welcome screen appears

---

## Step 2: Create an Account

### 2.1 Choose Method

On the welcome screen:
- **Sign Up**: Create a new account
- **Sign In**: If you already have an account

### 2.2 Registration

**Option 1: Email**
1. Tap "Sign Up"
2. Select "Email"
3. Enter your email
4. Create a secure password
5. Tap "Create account"

**Option 2: Google**
1. Tap "Continue with Google"
2. Select your Google account
3. Authorize permissions

**Option 3: Apple (iOS)**
1. Tap "Continue with Apple"
2. Use Face ID or Touch ID
3. Confirm

### 2.3 Email Verification

If you signed up with email:
1. Check your email inbox
2. Open the Campbnb Québec email
3. Click the verification link
4. Return to the app

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

### 3.1 Basic Information

The app guides you to complete your profile:

1. **Profile photo**
- Take a photo or choose one
- A clear photo increases trust

2. **First and last name**
- Enter your full name
- Visible to hosts during reservations

3. **Phone** (optional but recommended)
- Facilitates communication with hosts
- For important notifications

4. **Bio** (optional)
- Briefly introduce yourself
- E.g.: "Camping and nature enthusiast"

### 3.2 Preferences

Configure your preferences:
- **Language**: French or English
- **Notifications**: Enable important notifications
- **Theme**: Light or dark

## Step 5: First Search

### 5.1 Explore Home

1. On the home screen, explore recommended campsites
2. Tap a campsite to see details
3. Familiarize yourself with the interface

### 5.2 Try Search

1. Tap the **"Search"** tab
2. Type a destination: E.g., "Québec"
3. Explore results
4. Use filters to refine

### 5.3 Intelligent Search

Test intelligent search with AI:
1. Tap "Intelligent Search"
2. Type: "Lakeside campsite for fishing"
3. Discover personalized suggestions

---

## ️ Step 6: Explore the Map

### 6.1 Access Map

1. Tap the **"Map"** tab
2. Campsites display on the map
3. Explore different regions

### 6.2 Interact with Map

- **Zoom**: Pinch to zoom
- **Move**: Drag to explore
- **Markers**: Tap a marker to see details
- **Filters**: Use filters directly on the map

---

## Step 7: Discover Features

### 7.1 Messaging

1. Tap the **"Messages"** tab
2. Discover the messaging interface
3. You can contact hosts from listings

### 7.2 Profile

1. Tap the **"Profile"** tab
2. Explore your options:
- My reservations
- Favorites
- Settings
- Become a host

### 7.3 Settings

1. Go to Profile > Settings
2. Explore options:
- Notifications
- Privacy
- Language
- Security

---

## Step 8: Recommended First Steps

### 8.1 Add Favorites

1. Explore some campsites
2. Tap the ️ icon to add to favorites
3. Find them in Profile > Favorites

### 8.2 Contact a Host

1. Open a campsite that interests you
2. Tap "Contact host"
3. Send a test message
4. Familiarize yourself with messaging

### 8.3 Configure Notifications

1. Go to Settings > Notifications
2. Enable important notifications:
- New reservation requests
- Messages
- Confirmations
- Reminders
