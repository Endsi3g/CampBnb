## üöÄ Step 1: Activate Host Mode

### 1.1 Access Settings

1. Open the Campbnb Qu√©bec app
2. Sign in to your account
3. Tap the **"Profile"** tab
4. Tap **"Settings"**

### 1.2 Discover Host Mode

1. In settings, find **"Become a host"**
2. Read information about being a host
3. Discover benefits:
   - Generate income
   - Meet travelers
   - Share your love of camping

### 1.3 Activate

1. Tap **"Activate host mode"**
2. Confirm your choice
3. Host onboarding process begins

## ‚úÖ Step 2: Account Verification

### 2.1 Email Verification

If your email is not yet verified:
1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Return to the app

### 2.2 Identity Verification (Recommended)

To increase trust:
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Tap "Verify my identity"
3. Follow instructions
4. Upload required documents

### 2.3 Phone Number

1. Add your phone number
2. Verify via SMS
3. Facilitates communication with guests

---

## üë§ Step 3: Complete Host Profile

### 3.1 Basic Information

**Full name**
- Verify your name is correct
- Visible to guests

**Host bio**
Write a welcoming introduction:
- "Camping enthusiast for 20 years"
- "I love sharing my love of nature"
- "Warm welcome guaranteed"

**Profile photo**
- Make sure you have a clear photo
- A professional photo increases trust

### 3.2 Contact Information

**Phone**
- Add your number
- For urgent communications

**Email**
- Verify your email is correct
- For important notifications

### 3.3 Bank Information

To receive payments:
1. Tap "Add bank account"
2. Enter your information:
   - Account number
   - Transit code
   - Bank name
3. Verify information
4. Tap "Save"

**Note**: Payments are secured via Stripe.

---

## üìù Step 4: Create Your First Listing

### 4.1 Access Creation

1. From your profile, tap **"Host Dashboard"**
2. Tap **"Create listing"**
3. Creation form appears

### 4.2 Basic Information

**Title**
- Create a catchy title
- Maximum 60 characters
- E.g.: "Lakeside campsite with stunning view"

**Description**
- Describe your campsite in detail
- Minimum 100 characters
- Use "Generate with AI" button for optimized description

**Campsite type**
- Select: Tent, RV, Ready-to-Camp, or Wild

### 4.3 Location

1. Enter your full address
2. Verify location on map
3. Adjust if necessary

### 4.4 Capacity and Amenities

**Capacity**
- Maximum number of guests
- Number of bedrooms, beds, bathrooms

**Amenities**
- Check available amenities
- WiFi, Shower, Toilets, Fire Pit, etc.

### 4.5 Rules and Policies

**House rules**
- Write rules to follow
- E.g.: "No noise after 10pm"

**Cancellation policy**
- Choose: Flexible, Moderate, or Strict
- **Tip**: Start with "Flexible"

**Check-in/check-out times**
- Define arrival and departure times

### 4.6 Pricing

**Base price**
- Set your price per night
- Analyze competition

**Additional fees**
- Cleaning fee (optional)
- Service fee (automatically calculated)

**Discounts**
- Weekly discount
- Monthly discount

### 4.7 Photos

**Cover photo**
- Choose your best photo
- Will appear in search results

**Gallery**
- Add minimum 5 photos
- Amenity photos
- Environment photos

### 4.8 Calendar

1. Configure your availability
2. Block unavailable dates
3. Define availability rules

---

## üìÖ Step 5: Publish Your Listing

### 5.1 Preview

1. Tap "Preview"
2. Verify all information
3. Correct if necessary

### 5.2 Publication

1. When everything is correct, tap **"Publish"**
2. Your listing is submitted for moderation
3. You'll receive a notification once approved

**Moderation time**: Generally 24-48 hours

---

## üéâ Step 6: After Publication

### 6.1 Listing Status

Your listing can have different statuses:
- **Pending**: Under moderation
- **Active**: Visible and bookable
- **Inactive**: Temporarily disabled

### 6.2 First Request

Once your listing is active:
1. You'll receive a **notification** for each request
2. You have **24 hours** to respond
3. **Accept or decline** according to your availability

### 6.3 Dashboard

Explore your dashboard:
- **Overview**: Statistics and revenue
- **Listings**: Manage your listings
- **Reservations**: Manage reservations
- **Messages**: Conversations with guests
