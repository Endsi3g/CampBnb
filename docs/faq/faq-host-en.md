## Become a Host

### How do I become a host?

1. Create a Campbnb Québec account
2. Go to Settings > Become a host
3. Complete your host profile
4. Create your first listing

### What are the requirements to become a host?

- Have an available camping space
- Comply with local regulations
- Be available to communicate with guests
- Have a verified account

### Are there fees to become a host?

No, becoming a host is **free**. Only a 3% commission is charged on each reservation.

### Do I need to pay registration fees?

No, there are no registration or subscription fees.

## Creating Listings

### How do I create a listing?

1. Go to Dashboard > Create listing
2. Fill in basic information
3. Add photos
4. Set your rates
5. Configure your calendar
6. Publish your listing

### How many listings can I create?

There is no limit to the number of listings you can create.

### How long does it take to create a listing?

Approximately 15-30 minutes for a complete listing with all information.

### Can I use AI to generate my description?

Yes! Use the "Generate with AI" feature to automatically create an optimized description.

### What photos should I include?

- **Cover photo**: The most eye-catching
- **Amenity photos**: Shower, toilets, fire pit, etc.
- **Environment photos**: View, surrounding nature
- **Minimum 5 photos** recommended

### How do I set my prices?

- Analyze competitor prices
- Consider your amenities and location
- Adjust according to season
- Use dynamic pricing

## Managing Reservations

### How long do I have to respond to a request?

You have **24 hours** to accept or decline a request. After this time, the request is automatically cancelled.

### What happens if I decline a request?

- Guest is notified
- No charges are made to the guest
- Your acceptance rate may be affected

### Can I suggest alternative dates?

Yes, if requested dates don't work, you can suggest alternative dates to the guest.

### How do I manage my calendar?

1. Go to Dashboard > Calendar
2. Mark available/unavailable dates
3. Block dates for maintenance
4. Sync with other calendars if needed

### Can I cancel a confirmed reservation?

Yes, but this may affect your rating and visibility. Contact support if necessary.

### What should I do if a guest doesn't show up?

1. Contact the guest via messaging
2. Wait 24 hours after check-in time
3. Contact support if necessary

## Payments and Revenue

### How am I paid?

Payments are made via Stripe:
- **Timing**: 24-48 hours after guest check-in
- **Method**: Bank transfer or Stripe account

### What is the platform commission?

The commission is **3%** of the total reservation amount (excluding taxes).

### When will I receive my payment?

- **After check-in**: 24-48 hours after guest arrival
- **Automatic payment**: No need to request

### How do I set up my bank information?

1. Go to Settings > Payments
2. Add your bank account
3. Verify your information
4. Payments will be automatic

### Are there transaction fees?

No, transaction fees are covered by Campbnb. You receive the net amount (minus 3% commission).

### Can I offer discounts?

Yes, you can:
- Offer weekly discounts
- Offer monthly discounts
- Create seasonal promotions

## Optimization

### How do I improve my visibility?

1. **Quality photos**: Invest in great photos
2. **Detailed description**: Be precise and welcoming
3. **Quick response**: Respond within 2 hours
4. **High acceptance rate**: Accept legitimate requests
5. **Positive reviews**: Offer an excellent experience

### How do I increase my reservations?

- **Competitive prices**: Analyze the market
- **Availability**: Keep your calendar up to date
- **Exceptional photos**: First impression matters
- **Optimized description**: Use relevant keywords
- **Quick response**: Hosts who respond quickly get more reservations

### How do I improve my rating?

- **Offer exceptional experience**
- **Communicate clearly** before and during stay
- **Keep campsite clean**
- **Honor your commitments**: Dates, price, amenities
- **Respond to reviews**: Show your commitment

### Should I use dynamic pricing?

Dynamic pricing can help you:
- Maximize revenue in high season
- Attract more reservations in low season
- Optimize your occupancy rate

## Issues and Support

### I'm not receiving requests

Check:
- Your listing is active
- Your prices are competitive
- Your photos are quality
- Your description is complete
- Your availability is up to date

### A guest caused damage

1. **Take photos** of the damage
2. **Contact the guest** via messaging
3. **Contact support** with evidence
4. We will help you resolve the issue

### Payment issue

1. Check your bank information
2. Check your payment history
3. Contact support if necessary

### How do I report a problematic guest?

1. Go to the guest's profile
2. Tap "Report"
3. Describe the problem
4. Our team will review the report

### How do I contact support?

- **Email**: support@campbnb.quebec
- **Chat**: Available in the app
- **Phone**: (coming soon)
