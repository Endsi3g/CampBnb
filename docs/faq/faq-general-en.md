## ️ About Campbnb Québec

### What is Campbnb Québec?

Campbnb Québec is a campsite booking platform that allows campsite owners to rent their space and travelers to easily book their camping stay in Québec.

### How does Campbnb Québec work?

1. **Hosts** create listings for their campsites
2. **Guests** search and book campsites
3. **The platform** facilitates communication and secure payments

### What types of campsites are available?

- **Tent**: Tent sites
- **RV**: Recreational vehicle sites
- **Ready-to-Camp**: Campsites with included amenities
- **Wild**: Wilderness campsites

### Where is Campbnb Québec available?

Currently, Campbnb Québec is available in Québec, Canada. We plan to expand to other Canadian provinces.

## Account and Authentication

### How do I create an account?

1. Download the Campbnb Québec app
2. Tap "Sign Up"
3. Choose your method:
- Email and password
- Google
- Apple (iOS)

### Do I need to verify my account?

Verification is not mandatory but recommended to:
- Increase trust from hosts/guests
- Access certain features
- Secure your account

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login screen
2. Tap "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Follow instructions in the received email

### Can I have multiple accounts?

No, only one account per person is allowed. This ensures platform security and transparency.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Tap "Delete my account"
3. Confirm deletion

**Note**: This action is irreversible. All your data will be deleted.

## Platform Features

### What are the main features?

- **Intelligent search**: AI-powered search to find the ideal campsite
- **Interactive map**: Explore campsites on a map
- **Messaging**: Communicate directly with hosts/guests
- **Reviews and ratings**: Share your experiences
- **Secure payments**: Secure payment via Stripe
- **Notifications**: Stay informed in real-time

### How does intelligent search work?

Intelligent search uses Gemini AI to:
- Understand your needs in natural language
- Suggest personalized campsites
- Analyze your preferences
- Propose alternatives

**Example**: "Campsite near a lake for fishing with shower"

### What is the interactive map?

The interactive map allows you to:
- Visualize all available campsites
- See their exact location
- Filter by type, price, amenities
- Get directions

### How does messaging work?

- Real-time messages
- Instant notifications
- Conversation history
- Available in the app

### How do reviews work?

After a stay, guests can:
- Rate the campsite (1-5 stars)
- Leave a detailed comment
- Hosts can respond to reviews

## Security and Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we use:
- **HTTPS encryption**: All communications are encrypted
- **Secure authentication**: Via Supabase Auth
- **Secure payments**: Via Stripe (PCI-DSS certified)
- **Data protection**: GDPR compliant

### Who can see my information?

- **Public profile**: Name, photo, bio (visible to all)
- **Private information**: Email, phone (only for confirmed reservations)
- **Payments**: Bank information (never shared)

### How do I report suspicious behavior?

1. Go to the user's profile
2. Tap "Report"
3. Describe the problem
4. Our team will review the report

### What to do in case of a security issue?

Contact support immediately:
- **Email**: security@campbnb.quebec
- **Emergency**: support@campbnb.quebec

## Pricing

### How much does it cost to use Campbnb Québec?

Using the platform is **free** for guests and hosts.

**Fees for guests:**
- Campsite price (set by host)
- Service fee (10% of total)
- Applicable taxes

**Fees for hosts:**
- Platform commission (3% of reservation amount)

### How are fees calculated?

- **Base price**: Set by host
- **Cleaning fee**: Optional, set by host
- **Service fee**: 10% of total (excluding taxes)
- **Taxes**: According to local regulations

### When will I be charged?

- **Guests**: After reservation confirmation by host
- **Hosts**: Commission fees are deducted from payments
