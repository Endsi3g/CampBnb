## Reservations

### How do I book a campsite?

1. Search for a campsite that interests you
2. Select your stay dates
3. Indicate the number of guests
4. Review the total price
5. Tap "Book"
6. Wait for host confirmation (maximum 24 hours)

### Can I book for someone else?

Yes, you can book for other people. Make sure to indicate the correct number of guests.

### How long does the host have to respond?

The host has **24 hours** to accept or decline your request. After this time, the request is automatically cancelled.

### What happens if the host declines my request?

- No charges will be made
- You will receive a notification
- You can search for another campsite

### Can I modify my reservation after confirmation?

This depends on the campsite's cancellation policy:
- **Flexible**: Modifications possible up to 24 hours before
- **Moderate**: Modifications possible up to 5 days before
- **Strict**: Limited modifications

Contact the host to discuss modifications.

### Can I book multiple campsites at the same time?

Yes, you can have multiple simultaneous reservations, provided the dates don't overlap.

## Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?

- **Credit cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Debit cards**: With Visa or Mastercard logo
- **Payments via Stripe**: Secure and certified

### When will I be charged?

You will be charged **after reservation confirmation by the host**, not at the time of the request.

### What does the total price include?

- **Base price**: Price per night × number of nights
- **Cleaning fee**: If applicable
- **Service fee**: 10% of total
- **Taxes**: Applicable taxes according to region

### Can I pay in installments?

No, payment is made in one go at the time of confirmation.

### How do I get a receipt?

An electronic receipt is automatically sent to your email after payment. You can also find it in your reservations.

### Are there hidden fees?

No, all fees are clearly indicated before booking:
- Price per night
- Cleaning fee (if applicable)
- Service fee (10%)
- Taxes

## ️ Stays

### How do I get access instructions?

Access instructions are sent:
- **By email**: After reservation confirmation
- **In the app**: In reservation details
- **By message**: Host can send them directly

### What should I do if I can't find the campsite?

1. Contact the host via messaging
2. Use the "Directions" function in the app
3. Call the host if a number is provided

### Can I arrive earlier or leave later?

This depends on availability and host agreement. Contact them in advance to ask.

### What should I do if there's a problem during my stay?

1. **Contact the host** immediately via messaging
2. **Take photos** if necessary
3. **Contact support** if the problem persists

### Can I invite additional people?

This depends on the campsite's capacity and host agreement. Contact the host before inviting additional people. Additional fees may apply.

### Are pets allowed?

This depends on each campsite's policy. Check in the listing details or contact the host.

## Cancellations and Refunds

### How do I cancel a reservation?

1. Go to "My Reservations"
2. Select the reservation to cancel
3. Tap "Cancel"
4. Confirm cancellation

### What is the cancellation policy?

Each campsite has its own policy:
- **Flexible**: Full refund up to 24 hours before
- **Moderate**: Full refund up to 5 days before
- **Strict**: 50% refund up to 7 days before

Check details when booking.

### When will I be refunded?

Refunds are processed within **5 to 10 business days** after cancellation, depending on your bank.

### Can I get a refund if I need to leave early?

This depends on the cancellation policy. Contact the host and support to discuss.

### What happens if the host cancels my reservation?

- You will be **fully refunded**
- You will receive a notification
- We will help you find an alternative campsite

## Issues and Support

### Host is not responding to my messages

1. Wait 24 hours for a response
2. Send a reminder message
3. Contact support if necessary

### The campsite doesn't match the description

1. **Contact the host** immediately
2. **Take photos** as proof
3. **Contact support** if the problem persists

We will review your case and help resolve the issue.

### I can't access the campsite

1. Check access instructions
2. Contact the host immediately
3. Contact support urgently if necessary

### Payment issue

1. Check that your card is valid
2. Check your balance
3. Contact your bank
4. Contact Campbnb support

### How do I report a security issue?

Contact immediately:
- **Email**: security@campbnb.quebec
- **Support**: support@campbnb.quebec

### How do I contact support?

- **Email**: support@campbnb.quebec
- **Chat**: Available in the app
- **Phone**: (coming soon)
