# Tutorial: First Reservation - Campbnb Québec

This tutorial guides you step by step to make your first reservation on Campbnb Québec.

## Objective

By the end of this tutorial, you will know how to:
- Create a Campbnb Québec account
- Search for a campsite that meets your needs
- Make a reservation
- Manage your reservation

## Prerequisites

- A phone or tablet (iOS or Android)
- Internet connection
- Valid credit or debit card
- About 15 minutes

---

## Step 1: Download the Application

### 1.1 Download

**For iOS:**
1. Open the App Store
2. Search for "Campbnb Québec"
3. Tap "Get" or "Install"

**For Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "Campbnb Québec"
3. Tap "Install"

### 1.2 Open the Application

1. Tap the Campbnb Québec icon on your home screen
2. The welcome screen appears

---

## Step 2: Create an Account

### 2.1 Choose Registration Method

On the welcome screen:
1. Tap **"Sign Up"**
2. Choose your method:
- **Email**: To create an account with email
- **Google**: To use your Google account
- **Apple**: To use your Apple account (iOS only)

### 2.2 Email Registration

If you choose email:
1. **Enter your email**: example@email.com
2. **Create a password**: Minimum 8 characters, including letters and numbers
3. **Confirm your password**
4. Tap **"Create account"**

### 2.3 Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox
2. Open the Campbnb Québec email
3. Click the verification link
4. Return to the app

### 2.4 Complete Profile

The app guides you to complete your profile:
1. **Profile photo**: Take or choose a photo
2. **First and last name**: Enter your full name
3. **Phone**: Add your number (optional but recommended)
4. **Bio**: Briefly introduce yourself (optional)

---

## Step 3: Discover the Application

### 3.1 Main Navigation

The app includes 5 main sections:
- ** Home**: Recommended campsites
- ** Search**: Search for a campsite
- **️ Map**: Explore on the map
- ** Messages**: Messaging
- ** Profile**: Your account

### 3.2 Onboarding (First Use)

If it's your first use:
1. The app introduces the main features
2. Follow the introduction screens
3. Tap "Get Started" to access the app

---

## Step 4: Search for a Campsite

### 4.1 Access Search

1. Tap the **"Search"** tab at the bottom of the screen
2. The search screen appears

### 4.2 Simple Search

**Method 1: Search by destination**
1. Tap the "Where do you want to go?" field
2. Type your destination: e.g., "Québec" or "Lac Saint-Jean"
3. Select the destination from suggestions

**Method 2: Voice search**
1. Tap the microphone icon
2. Say your destination: "Campsite near Québec"
3. The app transcribes and searches

### 4.3 Intelligent AI Search

For a more precise search:
1. Tap **"Intelligent Search"**
2. Type your request in natural language:
- "Lakeside campsite for fishing"
- "Campsite with shower for family of 4"
- "Isolated campsite for meditation"
3. AI analyzes your request and suggests campsites

### 4.4 Select Dates

1. Tap **"Dates"**
2. Select your **arrival date** (check-in)
3. Select your **departure date** (check-out)
4. Number of nights displays automatically

### 4.5 Indicate Number of Guests

1. Tap **"Guests"**
2. Select number of **adults**
3. Select number of **children** (if applicable)
4. Total displays

### 4.6 Launch Search

1. Review your search criteria
2. Tap **"Search"**
3. Results display

---

## Step 5: Explore Results

### 5.1 Campsite List

Results show:
- **Photo**: Main campsite photo
- **Title**: Campsite name
- **Location**: City and province
- **Price**: Price per night
- **Rating**: Average rating and number of reviews
- **Type**: Tent, RV, Ready-to-Camp, Wild

### 5.2 Filter Results

To refine your search:
1. Tap **"Filters"** at the top
2. Select your criteria:
- **Campsite type**: Tent, RV, etc.
- **Price**: Price range
- **Amenities**: WiFi, Shower, Toilets, etc.
- **Minimum rating**: Campsites with minimum rating
3. Tap **"Apply"**

### 5.3 View on Map

1. Tap the **"Map"** icon at top right
2. Campsites display on the map
3. Tap a marker to see details
4. Return to list by tapping **"List"**

---

## Step 6: View Campsite Details

### 6.1 Open a Campsite

1. Tap a campsite in results
2. Details screen appears

### 6.2 Available Information

Details screen shows:
- **Photo gallery**: Scroll to see all photos
- **Title and description**: Information about the campsite
- **Amenities**: List of available amenities
- **House rules**: Rules to follow
- **Location**: Map with exact location
- **Reviews**: Reviews and ratings from other guests
- **Host**: Host profile

### 6.3 Check Availability

1. Scroll to the **calendar**
2. Available dates are in **green**
3. Unavailable dates are **grayed out**
4. Verify your dates are available

### 6.4 Contact Host

If you have questions:
1. Tap **"Contact host"**
2. Type your message
3. Tap **"Send"**
4. Host will receive a notification

---

## Step 7: Make Reservation

### 7.1 Select Dates

1. On details screen, tap **"Book"**
2. Reservation screen appears
3. Review selected dates
4. Modify if necessary

### 7.2 Indicate Number of Guests

1. Tap **"Guests"**
2. Select number of **adults**
3. Select number of **children**
4. Verify number doesn't exceed capacity

### 7.3 Review Price

Total price includes:
- **Base price**: Price per night × number of nights
- **Cleaning fee**: If applicable
- **Service fee**: 10% of total
- **Taxes**: Applicable taxes

### 7.4 Add Message (Optional)

1. Tap **"Message for host"**
2. Type a personal message:
- "Hello, we're very excited to come!"
- "We're arriving around 3pm, is that possible?"
3. This message will be sent with your request

### 7.5 Add Payment Method

If it's your first reservation:
1. Tap **"Add card"**
2. Enter your card information:
- Card number
- Expiration date
- CVV
- Name on card
3. Tap **"Save"**

### 7.6 Confirm Reservation

1. Review all information:
- Correct dates
- Correct number of guests
- Total price
- Payment method
2. Read cancellation terms
3. Tap **"Confirm reservation"**

---

## Step 8: Wait for Confirmation

### 8.1 Request Sent

After confirmation:
1. A confirmation message appears
2. You receive a confirmation email
3. Your request is sent to the host

### 8.2 Response Time

- Host has **24 hours** to respond
- You'll receive a notification as soon as they respond
- You can track status in "My Reservations"

### 8.3 If Host Accepts

1. You receive a **confirmation notification**
2. A **confirmation email** is sent
3. Your **card is charged**
4. You receive **access instructions**

### 8.4 If Host Declines

1. You receive a **notification**
2. **No charges** are made
3. You can **search for another campsite**

---

## Step 9: Manage Your Reservation

### 9.1 Access My Reservations

1. Tap the **"Profile"** tab
2. Tap **"My Reservations"**
3. Your reservations display by category:
- **Upcoming**: Confirmed reservations
- **Pending**: Pending requests
- **Past**: History

### 9.2 View Details

1. Tap a reservation
2. View:
- Check-in/check-out dates and times
- Number of guests
- Total price
- Access instructions
- Host contact information

### 9.3 Contact Host

1. From reservation details
2. Tap **"Contact host"**
3. Send a message

### 9.4 Get Directions

1. From reservation details
2. Tap **"Directions"**
3. App opens your navigation app
4. Follow instructions

---

## Tips and Tricks

### For a Better Experience

1. **Book in advance**: Best campsites go fast
2. **Read reviews**: Other guests' reviews are very helpful
3. **Contact host**: Ask questions before booking
4. **Check availability**: Make sure dates are free

### Security

- **Never share** your payment information outside the app
- **Communicate only** via Campbnb messaging
- **Verify host profile** before booking

---

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving verification email

1. Check your spam folder
2. Verify email is correct
3. Request a new verification email

### I can't find available campsites

1. Try different dates
2. Widen your search area
3. Use fewer filters
4. Contact support

### Payment issue

1. Verify your card is valid
2. Check your balance
3. Contact your bank
4. Contact Campbnb support
