# Tutorial: Manage Listings - Campbnb Québec

This tutorial guides you to optimize and efficiently manage your campsite listings on Campbnb Québec.

## Objective

By the end of this tutorial, you will know how to:
- Edit an existing listing
- Manage availability and calendar
- Optimize prices
- Improve listing visibility

## Prerequisites

- Be a host on Campbnb Québec
- Have at least one listing created
- About 20 minutes

---

## Step 1: Access Your Listings

### 1.1 Host Dashboard

1. Open the Campbnb Québec app
2. Tap the **"Profile"** tab
3. Tap **"Host Dashboard"**
4. Your listings display

### 1.2 Listings List

You see:
- **Active listings**: Visible and bookable
- **Inactive listings**: Temporarily disabled
- **Pending listings**: Under moderation

---

## Step 2: Edit a Listing

### 2.1 Open a Listing

1. Tap the listing to edit
2. Tap **"Edit"** at top right
3. Edit form appears

### 2.2 Edit Information

You can edit:
- **Title**: Improve title if necessary
- **Description**: Update description
- **Photos**: Add or remove photos
- **Amenities**: Add or remove amenities
- **Rules**: Modify house rules
- **Price**: Adjust rates

### 2.3 Save Changes

1. After each modification, tap **"Save"**
2. Changes are applied immediately
3. Guests will see new information

---

## Step 3: Manage Availability

### 3.1 Access Calendar

1. From dashboard, tap **"Calendar"**
2. Or from a listing, tap **"Manage availability"**

### 3.2 Mark Available/Unavailable Dates

**Available dates (green)**
- By default, all dates are available
- No action needed

**Block dates (gray)**
1. Tap a date
2. Select **"Block"**
3. Date becomes unavailable

**Blocking reasons:**
- Maintenance
- Personal use
- Already reserved elsewhere
- Other reason

### 3.3 Recurring Availability

Configure recurring rules:
1. Tap **"Availability rules"**
2. Configure:
- **Days of week**: E.g., Available only on weekends
- **Periods**: E.g., Available only in summer
- **Minimum nights**: E.g., Minimum 2 nights

### 3.4 Sync with Other Calendars

If you use other platforms:
1. Go to **"Settings" > "Sync"**
2. Connect your other calendars
3. Reservations sync automatically

---

## Step 4: Optimize Prices

### 4.1 Base Price

**Adjust price per night**
1. Open your listing
2. Tap **"Pricing"**
3. Modify **"Price per night"**
4. Save

**Tips for setting price:**
- Analyze competitor prices
- Consider your amenities
- Adjust according to season
- Test different prices

### 4.2 Dynamic Pricing

Enable dynamic pricing to:
- **Automatically increase** in high season
- **Automatically reduce** in low season
- **Optimize according to demand**

**Configuration:**
1. Tap **"Dynamic pricing"**
2. Enable option
3. Configure rules:
- Maximum increase: E.g., +50%
- Maximum reduction: E.g., -20%
- Factors: Season, demand, events

### 4.3 Discounts

**Weekly discount**
1. Enable "Weekly discount"
2. Enter percentage: E.g., 10%
3. Automatically applies for 7+ nights

**Monthly discount**
1. Enable "Monthly discount"
2. Enter percentage: E.g., 20%
3. Automatically applies for 30+ nights

**Special promotions**
1. Create promotions for specific dates
2. Offer limited-time discounts
3. Attract more reservations

---

## Step 5: Improve Visibility

### 5.1 Quality Photos

**Replace photos**
1. Open your listing
2. Tap **"Photos"**
3. Tap a photo to replace it
4. Choose a new photo

**Add photos**
1. Tap **"Add photo"**
2. Take or choose a photo
3. Reorder by drag and drop

**Tips for better photos:**
- Take photos in natural light
- Show all amenities
- Include environment photos
- Eye-catching cover photo

### 5.2 Optimized Description

**Improve description**
1. Open your listing
2. Tap **"Description"**
3. Use relevant keywords:
- "Lake", "Forest", "Nature"
- "Family", "Romantic", "Adventure"
- "Fishing", "Hiking", "Relaxation"

**Use AI to optimize**
1. Tap **"Generate with AI"**
2. AI creates optimized description
3. Personalize according to your needs

### 5.3 Catchy Title

**Create a better title**
- Maximum 60 characters
- Include highlights:
- "Lakeside"
- "Stunning view"
- "All amenities"
- "Family welcome"

---

## Step 6: Manage Reservations

### 6.1 View Requests

1. From dashboard, tap **"Reservations"**
2. Pending requests display
3. You have **24 hours** to respond

### 6.2 Accept or Decline

**Accept a request**
1. Tap the request
2. Review details
3. Tap **"Accept"**
4. Guest is notified

**Decline a request**
1. Tap the request
2. Tap **"Decline"**
3. Add a reason (optional)
4. Guest is notified

**Suggest alternative dates**
1. If dates don't work
2. Tap **"Suggest dates"**
3. Select new dates
4. Guest can accept or decline

### 6.3 Manage Confirmed Reservations

**View details**
1. Tap a confirmed reservation
2. View:
- Dates and guests
- Guest contact information
- Total price
- Status

**Contact guest**
1. From reservation
2. Tap **"Contact"**
3. Send a message

**Cancel a reservation**
1. If necessary, tap **"Cancel"**
2. Confirm cancellation
3. Guest is refunded according to policy

---

## Step 7: Analyze Performance

### 7.1 Statistics

From dashboard, view:
- **Revenue**: Total revenue (month, year)
- **Reservations**: Number of reservations
- **Occupancy rate**: Percentage of booked dates
- **Average rating**: Your overall rating
- **Reviews**: Total number of reviews

### 7.2 Trends

Analyze:
- **Revenue by period**: Graphs and trends
- **Reservations by month**: Reservation calendar
- **Performance**: Comparison with market average

### 7.3 Improvements

Use statistics to:
- Identify most requested periods
- Adjust prices according to demand
- Improve your offering

## Tips and Best Practices

### To Maximize Reservations

1. **Respond quickly**: Within 2 hours maximum
2. **Maintain high acceptance rate**: Accept legitimate requests
3. **Keep calendar up to date**: Mark unavailable dates
4. **Offer excellent experience**: To collect positive reviews
5. **Optimize prices**: According to season and demand

### To Improve Rating

1. **Communicate clearly**: Before and during stay
2. **Keep campsite clean**: Prepare before each arrival
3. **Honor commitments**: Dates, price, amenities
4. **Respond to reviews**: Show your commitment

### Security

- **Verify guest profiles**
- **Communicate only** via platform
- **Report suspicious behavior**

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving requests

Check:
- Your listing is active
- Your prices are competitive
- Your photos are quality
- Your description is complete
- Your availability is up to date

### How do I increase my revenue?

- Adjust prices according to season
- Offer discounts for long stays
- Improve photos and description
- Respond quickly to requests

### My listing doesn't appear in results

- Check that listing is active
- Check your search filters
- Contact support if necessary
