# ️ Tutorial: Become a Host - Campbnb Québec

This tutorial guides you step by step to become a host and create your first listing on Campbnb Québec.

## Objective

By the end of this tutorial, you will know how to:
- Activate host mode
- Create your first listing
- Configure your host profile
- Publish your listing

## Prerequisites

- A Campbnb Québec account (guest)
- An available camping space
- Photos of your campsite
- About 30 minutes

---

## Step 1: Activate Host Mode

### 1.1 Access Settings

1. Open the Campbnb Québec app
2. Sign in to your account
3. Tap the **"Profile"** tab at the bottom
4. Tap **"Settings"**

### 1.2 Activate Host Mode

1. In settings, find **"Become a host"**
2. Tap **"Activate host mode"**
3. Read information about being a host
4. Tap **"Continue"**

### 1.3 Account Verification

To become a host, you must:
1. **Verify your email** (if not already done)
2. **Verify your identity** (optional but recommended)
3. **Add a phone number** (recommended)

---

## Step 2: Complete Host Profile

### 2.1 Basic Information

1. **Full name**: Verify your name is correct
2. **Phone**: Add your phone number
3. **Host bio**: Write a short introduction:
- "Camping enthusiast for 20 years"
- "I love sharing my love of nature"
- "Warm welcome guaranteed"

### 2.2 Bank Information

To receive payments:
1. Tap **"Add bank account"**
2. Enter your information:
- Account number
- Transit code
- Bank name
3. Verify information
4. Tap **"Save"**

**Note**: Payments are secured via Stripe.

### 2.3 Profile Photo

1. Make sure you have a **clear profile photo**
2. A professional photo increases trust

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Listing

### 3.1 Access Creation

1. From your profile, tap **"Host Dashboard"**
2. Tap **"Create listing"**
3. Creation form appears

### 3.2 Basic Information

**Title (required)**
1. Tap the "Title" field
2. Create a catchy title:
- "Lakeside campsite with stunning view"
- "Tent site in the wilderness"
- "Family campsite with all amenities"
3. Maximum 60 characters

**Description (required)**
1. Tap the "Description" field
2. Describe your campsite in detail:
- Presentation of the place
- Highlights
- Environment
- Experience offered
3. Minimum 100 characters

**Tip**: Use the "Generate with AI" button to automatically create an optimized description.

### 3.3 Campsite Type

Select the type:
- **Tent**: Tent site
- **RV**: Recreational vehicle site
- **Ready-to-Camp**: Campsite with included amenities
- **Wild**: Wilderness campsite

### 3.4 Location

**Full address**
1. Tap "Address"
2. Enter your full address:
- Street number and name
- City
- Postal code
- Province
3. App automatically geolocates

**Map verification**
1. Verify location on map is correct
2. Adjust if necessary by moving marker

**Points of interest**
1. Mention nearby attractions:
- Lakes, rivers
- Hiking trails
- Nearby cities
- Available activities

---

## Step 4: Capacity and Amenities

### 4.1 Capacity

1. **Maximum number of guests**: Select (1-20)
2. **Number of bedrooms**: If applicable (0-10)
3. **Number of beds**: Select (1-20)
4. **Number of bathrooms**: Select (0-5)

### 4.2 Amenities

Check available amenities:
- **WiFi**: Internet connection
- **Shower**: Shower available
- **Toilets**: Toilets available
- **Fire Pit**: Fire pit for fires
- **Potable water**: Potable water available
- **Electricity**: Electrical outlet
- **Parking**: Parking space
- **Pets allowed**: If you accept pets

Add other amenities if necessary.

---

## Step 5: Rules and Policies

### 5.1 House Rules

Write rules to follow:
- "No noise after 10pm"
- "Respect nature and environment"
- "Smoking only in designated areas"
- "Pets must be on leash"

### 5.2 Cancellation Policy

Choose your policy:
- **Flexible**: Full refund up to 24 hours before
- **Moderate**: Full refund up to 5 days before
- **Strict**: 50% refund up to 7 days before

**Tip**: Start with "Flexible" to attract more guests.

### 5.3 Check-in/Check-out Times

1. **Check-in time**: E.g., 3:00 PM
2. **Check-out time**: E.g., 11:00 AM
3. Indicate if you're flexible

---

## Step 6: Pricing

### 6.1 Base Price

1. Tap "Price per night"
2. Enter your price: E.g., $50.00
3. **Tip**: Analyze competitor prices

### 6.2 Additional Fees

**Cleaning fee (optional)**
1. Enable "Cleaning fee"
2. Enter amount: E.g., $10.00

**Service fee**
- Automatically calculated (10% of total)
- You don't need to configure anything

### 6.3 Discounts

**Weekly discount**
1. Enable "Weekly discount"
2. Enter percentage: E.g., 10%
3. Automatically applies for 7+ night stays

**Monthly discount**
1. Enable "Monthly discount"
2. Enter percentage: E.g., 20%
3. Automatically applies for 30+ night stays

---

## Step 7: Photos

### 7.1 Cover Photo

1. Tap "Add cover photo"
2. Choose your **best photo**
3. This photo will appear in search results

### 7.2 Photo Gallery

Add minimum **5 photos**:
1. **Cover photo**: Eye-catching general view
2. **Amenity photos**: Shower, toilets, fire pit
3. **Environment photos**: View, surrounding nature
4. **Detail photos**: Living spaces, facilities

**Tips for great photos:**
- Take photos in **natural light**
- Show **different angles** of the campsite
- Include **people** for scale (optional)
- Avoid blurry or dark photos

### 7.3 Photo Order

1. Reorganize photos by drag and drop
2. First photo will be cover photo
3. Best photos first

---

## Step 8: Calendar and Availability

### 8.1 Configure Calendar

1. Tap "Calendar"
2. By default, all dates are available
3. **Block unavailable dates**:
- Maintenance dates
- Personal dates
- Dates already reserved elsewhere

### 8.2 Default Availability

Configure:
- **Available days of week**: E.g., All days
- **Check-in/check-out times**: Already configured

---

## Step 9: Preview and Publish

### 9.1 Preview

1. Tap "Preview"
2. Verify all information is correct:
- Title and description
- Photos
- Price
- Location
- Amenities

### 9.2 Corrections

If you find errors:
1. Return to the relevant step
2. Modify information
3. Return to preview

### 9.3 Publication

1. When everything is correct, tap **"Publish"**
2. Your listing is submitted for moderation
3. You'll receive a notification once approved

**Moderation time**: Generally 24-48 hours

---

## Step 10: After Publication

### 10.1 Listing Status

Your listing can have different statuses:
- **Pending**: Under moderation
- **Active**: Visible and bookable
- **Inactive**: Temporarily disabled
- **Rejected**: Requires modifications

### 10.2 First Reservation

Once your listing is active:
1. You'll receive **notifications** for each request
2. You have **24 hours** to respond
3. **Accept or decline** according to your availability

### 10.3 Optimization

To improve your visibility:
- **Respond quickly** to requests
- **Keep your calendar up to date**
- **Collect positive reviews**
- **Improve your photos** if necessary

---

## Tips and Best Practices

### To Attract More Guests

1. **Exceptional photos**: Invest in photography
2. **Detailed description**: Be precise and welcoming
3. **Competitive prices**: Analyze the market
4. **Quick response**: Respond within 2 hours
5. **Availability**: Keep your calendar up to date

### To Offer an Excellent Experience

1. **Prepare the campsite** before arrival
2. **Communicate clearly** instructions
3. **Be available** during stay
4. **Honor your commitments**: Dates, price, amenities

---

## Troubleshooting

### My listing was rejected

1. Check rejection reasons in notifications
2. Fix identified issues
3. Resubmit

### I'm not receiving requests

- Check that your listing is active
- Check your prices (maybe too high)
- Improve your photos and description
- Check your availability

### Payment issue

1. Check your bank information
2. Contact support if necessary
